Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Project Name
AI Food Concierge – Smart Food Decision Web App

1. Product Overview
Product Vision
Help people decide what to eat in under 30 seconds by replacing endless restaurant browsing with intelligent, personalized recommendations based on mood, preferences, and context.
Product Mission
Reduce decision fatigue by acting as an AI food concierge that understands users before recommending where and what they should eat.

2. Problem Statement
The Problem
Many people struggle with one simple daily question:
"What should I eat today?"
Although platforms like Google Maps, Foodpanda, and GrabFood provide thousands of restaurant options, they require users to manually compare restaurants, menus, ratings, and reviews.
Instead of making the decision easier, the abundance of choices creates choice overload, leading to decision fatigue.
As a result, users often:
    • 
Spend 10–20 minutes deciding what to eat
    • 
    • 
Repeatedly choose the same meals
    • 
    • 
Buy unhealthy fast food because it's easier
    • 
    • 
Skip meals altogether
    • 
    • 
Experience frustration when eating with friends because nobody can decide
    • 
Current food discovery platforms answer:
"What restaurants are nearby?"
They do not answer:
"What do I actually feel like eating right now?"
Our product bridges this gap.

12. Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
Traditional food apps focus on searching for restaurants, requiring users to manually browse and compare options. Our AI Food Concierge focuses on making the decision. By combining mood analysis, personal preferences, contextual filters, and AI-driven recommendations, the app eliminates decision fatigue and helps users confidently choose what and where to eat in seconds, making the dining experience faster, more personalized, and less overwhelming.
